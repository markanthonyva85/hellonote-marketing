3-5 bullets. Clinical and actionable. Specific not generic.
The "save this post" moment.

Example for CPT 97110:
- Bill 97110 for isolated exercises with one outcome — strength, endurance, ROM, or flexibility
- Document the specific exercise, sets, reps, resistance, and the functional goal
- 8-minute rule: need at least 8 minutes to bill 1 unit
- If activity is functional and multi-outcome — use 97530 not 97110
