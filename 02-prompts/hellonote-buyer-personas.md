## WRITING RULES WHEN USING THIS PERSONA

1. Always lead with their reality before HelloNote's features
2. Use peer voice — write like a fellow therapist, not a software company
3. Keep CTAs under 3 words — "Start Free" beats "Get Started Today"
4. Be specific — "4,000+ notes documented monthly" beats "thousands of users"
5. Never use corporate jargon — "leverage", "optimize", "solution" are banned
6. Always address the no-contracts, no-setup-fees message — it removes friction
7. Reference the therapist-built origin story whenever possible — it's the deepest trust signal
