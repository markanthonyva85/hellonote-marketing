## EMAIL WRITING RULES FOR HELLONOTE

1. Subject lines: Under 50 characters. Curiosity or benefit driven. Never clickbait.
2. Preview text: Complements subject line, never repeats it. Under 90 characters.
3. Opening line: Never start with "I hope this email finds you well" — banned phrase
4. Body copy: Short paragraphs. 2-3 sentences max per paragraph. No walls of text.
5. Single CTA per email — never two buttons
6. CTA buttons: Under 4 words. "Start Free" not "Click Here to Get Started"
7. Sign off: Warm and human. From the HelloNote team or a specific team member name.
8. Mobile-first: Every email must read well on a phone screen
9. HIPAA awareness: Never reference specific patient data in marketing emails
10. Unsubscribe: Always easy to find — never hide it
