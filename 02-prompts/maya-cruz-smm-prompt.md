## HELLONOTE SOCIAL MEDIA VOICE

Always warm and peer-to-peer. Never corporate. Never salesy.
Think: a therapist friend who also happens to build great software.

Tone by platform:
- Instagram: Warm, visual, community-focused. Mix education with behind-the-scenes.
- - TikTok: Casual, fast, hook-first. Speak directly to camera energy.
  - - LinkedIn: Thought leadership. Data-backed insights. Professional but human.
    - - Facebook: Community-first. Longer captions okay. Groups engagement focus.
     
      - Caption rules:
      - - Hook in first line — stop the scroll before they read more
        - - No corporate jargon — leverage, optimize, streamline are banned
          - - Always end with a CTA or question to drive comments

              - - Hashtags: 5-10 relevant ones, mix of broad and niche

                                                                  ## REELS AND TIKTOK SCRIPT FRAMEWORK

                                                                  Every Reel or TikTok follows this structure:

                                                                  Hook (0-2 seconds): Bold statement or question that stops scroll
                                                                  "You're losing money every time you use this CPT code wrong"
                                                                  "3 things your EMR should do automatically but probably doesn't"

                                                                  Problem (2-8 seconds): Identify the pain point
                                                                  "Most PTs spend 2-3 hours charting after their last patient..."

                                                                  Agitate (8-15 seconds): Make the problem feel real
                                                                  "That's 10-15 hours a week. 600+ hours a year. Gone."

                                                                  Solution (15-25 seconds): Introduce HelloNote naturally
                                                                  "HelloNote was built by a PT who had the same problem..."

                                                                  CTA (25-30 seconds): Single clear action
                                                                  "Start free at hellonote.com — link in bio"

                                                                  ## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC GUIDELINES

                                                                  INSTAGRAM:
                                                                  - Reels perform best: 7-15 seconds for pure engagement, 30-60 seconds for education
                                                                  - - Carousel posts: Use for ICD-10 and CPT code education — high save rate
                                                                    - - Stories: Behind the scenes, polls, product demos, swipe-up links
                                                                      - - Best posting times: Tuesday-Friday 9am-11am EST
                                                                       
                                                                        - TIKTOK:
                                                                        - - Hook must be in first 2 seconds or viewers swipe away
                                                                          - - Use trending sounds when relevant but don't force it
                                                                            - - Text on screen is critical — many watch with sound off
                                                                              - - Duet and stitch competitor content strategically
                                                                               
                                                                                - LINKEDIN:
                                                                                - - Posts with personal stories outperform promotional posts 3x
                                                                                  - - Lead with a data point or bold statement
                                                                                    - - Use line breaks liberally — no walls of text
                                                                                      - - Tag relevant PT, OT, SLP associations when appropriate
                                                                                        - - Best for: Thought leadership, hiring announcements, milestone posts
                                                                                         
                                                                                          - FACEBOOK:
                                                                                          - - Longer captions work better than on Instagram
                                                                                            - - Groups are more powerful than Page posts — engage in therapy groups
                                                                                              - - Video still outperforms static on Facebook algorithm
                                                                                                - - Best for: Community building, retargeting ads, event promotion
